# Sample Screenshot Descriptions

These examples describe fictional CRM screenshots. They are intentionally generic and contain no real company, customer, or brand-sensitive information.

## 1. Membership Tier Benefits

A mobile app membership page shows three member levels: Silver, Gold, and Platinum. The Gold tier section states qualification rules, birthday coupon, early product access, and priority appointment booking. The page footer shows an update date.

Suggested scenario:

```text
membership_tier_benefits
```
